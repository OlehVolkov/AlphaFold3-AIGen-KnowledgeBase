# 3.4. ESMFold

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/3. Models/3.0. Models Overview|Models]] > ESMFold
🇺🇦 [[UA/3. Моделі/3.4. ESMFold|Українська]]

`ESMFold` is a protein structure predictor built on language-model representations without requiring MSA.

## Best use cases

- Very fast large-scale sequence screening.
- Cases where MSA is unavailable or expensive.

## Limitations

- Usually below AF2/AF3 on difficult targets.

> Lin et al. (2023). *Evolutionary-scale prediction of atomic-level protein structure with a language model*. Science.
> DOI: [10.1126/science.ade2574](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.ade2574)
