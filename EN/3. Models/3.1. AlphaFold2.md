# 3.1. AlphaFold2

[[EN/1. AlphaFold3/1.0. Home|Home]] > [[EN/3. Models/3.0. Models Overview|Models]] > AlphaFold2
🇺🇦 [[UA/3. Моделі/3.1. AlphaFold2|Українська]]

`AlphaFold2` is a deep learning model for predicting protein 3D structure from sequence.

## Best use cases

- High-accuracy monomer structure prediction.
- Structural interpretation of sequence variants.

## Limitations

- Not designed as a unified model for ligand/nucleic-acid complexes.
- Depends on MSA quality and depth.

> Jumper et al. (2021). *Highly accurate protein structure prediction with AlphaFold*. Nature.
> DOI: [10.1038/s41586-021-03819-2](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-021-03819-2)
