# 3.2. AlphaFold3

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/3. Models/3.0. Models Overview|Models]] > AlphaFold3
🇺🇦 [[UA/3. Моделі/3.2. AlphaFold3|Українська]]

`AlphaFold3` extends AF2 toward multimolecular systems: proteins, RNA, DNA, ligands, and complexes.

## Best use cases

- Unified modeling across multiple biomolecular entity types.
- Benchmarking across docking/structure quality metrics.

## Limitations

- High compute requirements.
- Some cases require additional stereochemical validation.

> Abramson et al. (2024). *Accurate structure prediction of biomolecular interactions with AlphaFold 3*. Nature.
> DOI: [10.1038/s41586-024-07487-w](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-024-07487-w)
