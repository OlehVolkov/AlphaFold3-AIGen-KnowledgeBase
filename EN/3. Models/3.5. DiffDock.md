# 3.5. DiffDock

[[EN/1. AlphaFold3/1.0. Home|Home]] > [[EN/3. Models/3.0. Models Overview|Models]] > DiffDock
🇺🇦 [[UA/3. Моделі/3.5. DiffDock|Українська]]

`DiffDock` is a diffusion-based method for ligand pose prediction in protein binding pockets.

## Best use cases

- Docking workflows in early-stage drug discovery.
- Multi-pose candidate generation with uncertainty handling.

## Limitations

- Focused on ligand docking, not full multimolecular generality.

> Corso et al. (2022). *DiffDock: Diffusion Steps, Twists, and Turns for Molecular Docking*.
> DOI: [10.48550/arXiv.2210.01776](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2210.01776)
