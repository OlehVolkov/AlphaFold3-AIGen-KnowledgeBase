# 3.0. Models Overview

[[EN/1. AlphaFold3/1.0. Home|Home]] > Models
🇺🇦 [[UA/3. Моделі/3.0. Огляд моделей|Українська]]

Comparative overview of key models for structure prediction and docking.

| Model | Primary task | Strengths | Limitations |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[EN/3. Models/3.1. AlphaFold2|AlphaFold2]] | Protein monomers | Very high protein accuracy | Limited multimodal context |
| [[EN/3. Models/3.2. AlphaFold3|AlphaFold3]] | Protein/DNA/RNA/ligand complexes | Unified multimolecular modeling | High compute requirements |
| [[EN/3. Models/3.3. RoseTTAFold|RoseTTAFold]] | Proteins and complexes | Strong practical baseline | Often lower quality on harder complexes |
| [[EN/3. Models/3.4. ESMFold|ESMFold]] | Fast sequence-to-structure | No MSA, very fast | Lower accuracy on difficult cases |
| [[EN/3. Models/3.5. DiffDock|DiffDock]] | Ligand docking | Strong pose prediction | Not general for all complex types |
