# 3.3. RoseTTAFold

[[EN/1. AlphaFold3/1.0. Home|Home]] > [[EN/3. Models/3.0. Models Overview|Models]] > RoseTTAFold
🇺🇦 [[UA/3. Моделі/3.3. RoseTTAFold|Українська]]

`RoseTTAFold` is a three-track neural architecture integrating 1D/2D/3D representations for protein structure prediction.

## Best use cases

- Strong practical baseline in structure tasks.
- Independent cross-check against AF pipelines.

## Limitations

- Typically below newest models on harder complex benchmarks.

> Baek et al. (2021). *Accurate prediction of protein structures and interactions using a three-track neural network*. Science.
> DOI: [10.1126/science.abj8754](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.abj8754)
