# lDDT — Local Distance Difference Test

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/Index|Concepts]] > Structural Bioinformatics
🇺🇦 [[UA/2. Концепції/2.3. Структурна-Біоінформатика/2.3.2. lDDT|Українська]]

## Mathematical definition

lDDT evaluates local distance preservation without global superposition, usually using multiple distance cutoffs.

## Why it is useful

- robust to domain motion
- better local quality signal
- suitable for heterogeneous complexes

## pLDDT in AlphaFold

pLDDT is the predicted local confidence derived from lDDT concept.

### Common color scale in AF viewers

| pLDDT | Color | Interpretation |
|---:|---|---|
| >= 90 | blue | very high confidence |
| 70-90 | cyan | good |
| 50-70 | yellow | low |
| < 50 | orange/red | very low |

## lDDT by molecule type

lDDT-style confidence can be interpreted for proteins, nucleic acids, and interfaces.

## lDDT vs RMSD

Use lDDT for local confidence and RMSD for global deviation after alignment.

> Mariani et al. (2013). *lDDT: a local superposition-free score for comparing protein structures and models*. Bioinformatics.
> DOI: [10.1093/bioinformatics/btt473](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btt473)
