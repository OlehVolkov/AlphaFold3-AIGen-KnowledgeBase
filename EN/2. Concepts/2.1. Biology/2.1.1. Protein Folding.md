# Protein Folding

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/Index|Concepts]] > Biology
🇺🇦 [[UA/2. Концепції/2.1. Біологія/2.1.1. Згортання білків|Українська]]

Protein folding is the process by which an amino acid sequence acquires a stable 3D conformation with low free energy.

$$\Delta G_{fold} = \Delta H - T\Delta S$$

AF3 models structure directly in coordinate space and can represent both ordered and disordered regions.
