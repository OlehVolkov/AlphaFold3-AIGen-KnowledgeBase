# Ligands and Small Molecules

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/Index|Concepts]] > Biology
🇺🇦 [[UA/2. Концепції/2.1. Біологія/2.1.3. Ліганди та малі молекули|Українська]]

Ligands are small molecules that bind to biomolecular targets, often in specific pockets.

Relevant properties include affinity, geometry, protonation state, and stereochemistry.
