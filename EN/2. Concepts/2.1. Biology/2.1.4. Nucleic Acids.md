# Nucleic Acids

DNA and RNA are sequence polymers with strong structure-function coupling.

## Structural organization

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    NA[Nucleic acids] --> DNA[DNA]
    NA --> RNA[RNA]
    DNA --> D1[Double helix]
    DNA --> D2[Major/minor grooves]
    RNA --> R1[Single-stranded bias]
    RNA --> R2[Complex secondary structures]
```

## DNA vs RNA key differences

| Property | DNA | RNA |
|---|---|---|
| Sugar | Deoxyribose | Ribose |
| Base | Thymine | Uracil |
| Typical form | Double helix | Single-stranded with folds |
| Stability | Higher | Lower |

## RNA secondary structure

Common motifs: hairpins, internal loops, bulges, pseudoknots.
