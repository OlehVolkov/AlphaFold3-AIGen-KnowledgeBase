# 4.3. AlphaFoldDB

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/4. Datasets/4.0. Datasets Overview|Datasets]] > AlphaFoldDB
🇺🇦 [[UA/4. Датасети/4.3. AlphaFoldDB|Українська]]

`AlphaFoldDB` provides proteome-scale predicted protein structures.

## Key properties

- Large-scale access to predicted structures.
- Confidence fields including pLDDT.
- Integrated within EMBL-EBI resources.

## Typical use

- Fast structural screening.
- Prioritization for downstream experimental validation.

> AlphaFold Protein Structure Database: [https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk](https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk)
