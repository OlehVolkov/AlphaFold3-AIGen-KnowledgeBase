# 4.1. PDB

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/4. Datasets/4.0. Datasets Overview|Datasets]] > PDB
🇺🇦 [[UA/4. Датасети/4.1. PDB|Українська]]

`PDB` (Protein Data Bank) is the primary open repository of experimental biomolecular 3D structures.

## Key properties

- Experimental methods: X-ray, cryo-EM, NMR.
- Formats: `mmCIF`, legacy `PDB`.
- Broad coverage of proteins, complexes, and ligands.

## Typical use

- Ground truth for structural model validation.
- Reference structures for comparative analysis.

> RCSB PDB (official portal): [https://www.rcsb.org](https://www.rcsb.org)
