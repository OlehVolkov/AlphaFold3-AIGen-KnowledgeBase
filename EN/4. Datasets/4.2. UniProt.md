# 4.2. UniProt

[[EN/1. AlphaFold3/1.0. Home|Home]] > [[EN/4. Datasets/4.0. Datasets Overview|Datasets]] > UniProt
🇺🇦 [[UA/4. Датасети/4.2. UniProt|Українська]]

`UniProt` is the central protein sequence and annotation knowledgebase.

## Key properties

- `UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot` (curated) and `UniProtKB/TrEMBL` (automated).
- Stable protein identifiers and cross-resource integration.
- API-based programmatic access.

## Typical use

- FASTA acquisition for modeling pipelines.
- Functional context for structural interpretation.

> UniProt (official portal): [https://www.uniprot.org](https://www.uniprot.org)
