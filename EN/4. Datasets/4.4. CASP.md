# 4.4. CASP

[[Home|Home]] > [[EN/4. Datasets/4.0. Datasets Overview|Datasets]] > CASP
🇺🇦 [[UA/4. Датасети/4.4. CASP|Українська]]

`CASP` (Critical Assessment of Structure Prediction) is the leading blind benchmark for structure prediction methods.

## Key properties

- Blind targets before public release of experimental structures.
- Standardized evaluation protocols.
- Recurring benchmark cycles.

## Typical use

- Independent quality assessment of new methods.
- SOTA comparison under controlled conditions.

> CASP (official portal): [https://predictioncenter.org](https://predictioncenter.org)
